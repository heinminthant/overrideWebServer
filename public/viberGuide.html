Viber Setup Guide

Step 1 - Creating Public Account

Open this link in a new table to create a public account

Fill in your phone number and press Login

A verification number will be sent to your viber account

Copy the number and paste it in the text field

Click Next

You will arrive at the create account page

Fill in the information

Then click Create

After bot is created, go to the bot.

Press the copy button on the Token text field.

You now have the access token. Go to your Telegram bot to set the token.

Step 2 - Get Token

At the Telegram Bot, click on Viber Menu

Click on Set Access Token

Type into the chat vtoken:(token you copied)

The token will be uploaded

The Viber Setup is finally complete
